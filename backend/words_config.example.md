# Word Configurations Overview

This document explains the reasoning behind each default word configuration provided in `words_config.example.json`.

1. **EMERGENCY (Priority 1)**: All three channels exceed large thresholds dynamically. This equates to a broad, full-force clenching or jaw-dropping action which hits the temporal, ear, and back jaw simultaneously. It indicates high urgency and triggers regardless of isolation.
2. **WATER (Priority 2)**: Only requires channel 2 (the chin) to exceed 2000. It is a targeted gesture like a forward swallow or lip stretch that isolates the primary lower throat muscles without strong triggering elsewhere.
3. **HELLO (Priority 3)**: Requires high v1 (back jaw) and specifically low v2 (chin). Thus this points to a smile or a jaw slide that isn't pulling on the central jaw.
4. **YES (Priority 4)**: High v3 but low v1. Like a brow raise or specific ear tension, not clenching the jaw.
5. **NO (Priority 5)**: High v1 but low v3. Clenching or sliding jaw tension, while relaxing upper brow lines.
