# Where to find the new features

I had our browser assistant navigate to your local dashboard to take a look, and everything is definitely there! 

The tabs you are looking for are not new Chrome/Edge windows. They are **two big buttons built directly into the UI**, right at the top-center of the screen under the connection status!

Here is a visual guide of where everything is located:

### 1. How to Switch Views (The "Tabs")
Look at the **top center** of your webpage. You will see two buttons:
- `Real-time Dashboard`
- `Words Configuration`

Click **"Words Configuration"** to open the screen where you can add new words, set their priorities, and define the sensor ranges.

### 2. Where is the Record Button?
The recording feature requires you to see the live data as you perform the muscle gesture, so it is located on the **Real-time Dashboard** tab!
- Scroll down slightly to the middle of the screen (right above the scrolling charts).
- Look for a large **Red "Start Recording"** button under the "High-Precision Recording" section.
- Hit Start, make your muscle gesture, and hit **Stop**. The exact Maximums, Minimums, and Analog Envelope values will instantly appear in a grid for you to copy over!

> [!TIP]
> If you truly do not see these buttons at the top of your screen, your browser is aggressively caching the old code! Try pressing **Ctrl + Shift + R** (or **Cmd + Shift + R** on Mac) to force a Hard Refresh and download the new code.
